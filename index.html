Classic Minerals Limited (ASX: CLZ) is pleased to announce the execution of a binding terms sheet with AuResources AG, pursuant to which AuResources will initially advance US$10 million (A$15.3 million) (1st Tranche) to Classic.

AuResources is a pioneering venture leveraging blockchain technology to revolutionize the precious metals market.

With our unique offering, producers can raise funds without equity dilution or accruing debt. Investors, on the other hand, can gain exposure to the gold market much earlier in its lifecycle and, at a discounted price.

Our tokens conform to the ERC20 standard, ensuring compatibility with various DeFi protocols.

Central to our operations is a commitment to Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) considerations.

Through our experience in West Africa as an exploration and operations development company via Prosperite Limited, we developed a robust Responsible Conduct Charter, ensuring that our producers adhere to sustainable and ethical practices, and implement blockchain-backed supply chain tracking for transparency and ethical sourcing.

The AuResources Platform delivers an unparalleled, intuitive, and streamlined solution tailored for commodity producers and investors.

Commodity producers can seamlessly manage token issuances and investors are equipped with sophisticated portfolio management tools and exclusive opportunities to participate in initial future offerings.
