Meet our team

Norman Peires

Co-founder and Chairman

Norman Peires, born and raised in Cape Town, has been in business for over 50 years with a passion for business. He founded Idiliq Hotels in London in 1979, which has grown into a globally respected hotel group with a significant portfolio of resort real estate owned debt-free by his family. See more about Idiliq here.

Norman is also committed to developing indigenous local populations and improving their quality of life. Since 2013, he has been engaged in gold mining in Mali and has invested $10 million of his family office funds to explore opportunities there.

Jason Kwan

Co-founder and Chief Financial Officer
